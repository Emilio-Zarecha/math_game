Three interactive diagrams for Intermediate Arithmetic — order of operations, fraction addition, and percent change.

1 — Order of Operations (PEMDAS)

Click each step to highlight the operation evaluated next. The expression 2 + 3 × (8 − 2²) ÷ 6 − 1 walks through all six PEMDAS levels.

2 + 3 × ( 8 − 2² ) ÷ 6 − 1
PEMDAS: Parentheses → Exponents → Multiplication & Division (left to right) → Addition & Subtraction (left to right)

2 — Fraction Addition — Area Model

Adjust the two fractions. The diagram converts both to a common denominator and shows the sum as shaded sections of equal-width strips.

3 — Percent Change — Before / After Bar

Set the original value and the new value to visualise the increase or decrease as a percentage.
